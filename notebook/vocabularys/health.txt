health
##
	health = the condition of the body and state of being well 
	EG:
	[
		[Physical, Mental, Preventavtive] health,		
		To be in [good, poor, declining, fragile] health,
		To [promote, boost, improve] your health
			Superfoods can boost your health
			Ultra-processed foods do not boost your health, they harm your health  	
		I'm in good health, but my grandad is sick and in declining health,
		I'm in poor health/ My health situation is not good
		I'm in good health -> I have never felt better/ I am alive and kicking
		I'm going downhill 
	]
	Synonyms: [well-being, wellness, fitness]
	Declining = going down, getting worse
